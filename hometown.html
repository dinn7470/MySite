Staten Island, USA

New York City
Richmond County
Between New Jersey and Brooklyn

Location

Staten Island is located just across the river from New Jersey.

About

Staten Island is one of the five boroughs of New York City. It is the least populated and most suburban.
It is the only borough of New York City besides Manhattan that is an Island.
It is the only NYC borough that isn't connected to the Subway.

Weather

The weather is pretty moderate and not very different from most of NorthEastern America

Population

About 500,000 people are residents of Staten Island.

Sports

There are no sports teams that play on Staten Island. All Staten Islanders root for the different Professional New York teams that play in other parts of the city.

Things To Do

  • Go to the mall
  • Hike in the trail parks
  • Go to the movies
